Parameter information

Model

Our model is a pair-formation Susceptible-Infected-Recovered (SIR) system of differential equations adapted from Betti, Farrell, and Heffernan (2022). Briefly, in pair formation models the infection is driven by the rate of partnership formation between individuals and transmission probabilities per sexual act within the partnership Kretzschmar and Heijne (2017). In these models, single individuals (individuals that don’t form partnerships) don’t get infected nor infect others. Individuals within partnerships only get infected with a probability \(\phi\) if the other individual within that partnership is infected already.

The variables of the model are specified in Table 1.

Table 1: Variables used in the model Equation 1.
Variable Definition
\(S\) Single susceptible individuals
\(I\) Single infected individuals
\(R\) Single recovered individuals
\(P_{SS}\) Partnership of two susceptible individuals
\(P_{SI}\) Partnership of one susceptible and one infected individuals
\(P_{SR}\) Partnership of one susceptible and one recovered individuals
\(P_{II}\) Partnership of two infected individuals
\(P_{IR}\) Partnership of one infected and one recovered individuals
\(P_{RR}\) Partnership of two recovered individuals

The differential equations representation for the model is: \[ \begin{align} \frac{dS}{dt} &= - \rho S + \sigma (2 P_{SS} + P_{SI} + P_{SR})\\ \frac{dI}{dt} &= - (\rho + \delta) I + \sigma (2P_{II} + P_{SI} + P_{IR}) \\ \frac{dR}{dt} &= - \rho R + \delta I + \sigma (2 P_{RR} + P_{SR} + P_{IR}) \\ \frac{dP_{SS}}{dt} &= \frac{1}{2}\rho \frac{S^2}{N} - \sigma P_{SS} \\ \frac{dP_{SI}}{dt} &= \rho (1 - h) \frac{SI}{N} - (\sigma + \phi h + \delta) P_{SI} \\ \frac{dP_{II}}{dt} &= \frac{1}{2}\rho \frac{I^2}{N} + \rho h \frac{SI}{N} + \phi h P_{SI} - (\sigma + 2\delta)P_{II} \\ \frac{dP_{SR}}{dt} &= \delta P_{SI} + \rho \frac{SR}{N} - \sigma P_{SR} \\ \frac{dP_{IR}}{dt} &= \rho \frac{IR}{N} + \delta P_{II} - (\sigma + \delta) P_{IR} \\ \frac{dP_{RR}}{dt} &= \delta P_{IR} + \frac{1}{2}\rho \frac{R^2}{N} - \sigma P_{RR} \\ \end{align} \tag{1}\]

With the parameters and their meanings are established in Table 2. As the model was fitted using a Bayesian framework, prior distributions for the parameters are also specified in Table 2.

Table 2: Parameters used in the model Equation 1.
Parameter Definition
\(\rho\) Partnership formation rate
\(\sigma\) Partnership dissolution rate
\(\delta\) Infection recovery rate
\(h\) Probability of transmission per contact
\(\phi\) Contact rate per partnership
